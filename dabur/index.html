About Dabur

Dabur India Ltd is one of India's leading FMCG companies with a legacy of over 135 years. Founded in 1884 by Dr. S.K. Burman, Dabur has grown to become a trusted name in Ayurvedic healthcare.

Our products are based on the principles of Ayurveda and are developed through modern scientific research. We are committed to providing natural and effective solutions for health and wellness.

With a portfolio of over 250 herbal/Ayurvedic products, Dabur today operates in key consumer product categories like Hair Care, Oral Care, Health Care, Skin Care, Home Care and Foods.
